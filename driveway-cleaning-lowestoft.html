Driveway Cleaning in Lowestoft

Remove stains and prevent deterioration. Professional pressure washing tackles algae, oil, rust and weather damage.

The Problem

Different stains mean different problems. Algae traps moisture and degrades surfaces. Oil spreads if not treated quickly. Rust indicates corrosion beginning. Ignoring stains costs you money through accelerated damage.

Common Stain Types

  • Algae (black/green): Traps moisture, damages surface. Needs treatment + pressure wash.
  • Oil stains (dark spots): Soak deep, spread over time. Treated with degreasers.
  • Rust (orange/brown): Indicates corrosion problems. Needs specialist cleaners.
  • Tire marks & grime: Surface buildup. High-pressure washing removes.

Why This Matters

Professional cleaning: £150-300/year. Concrete replacement: £50-100+ per sq meter. Early cleaning prevents expensive repairs and extends driveway life 5-10 years.
