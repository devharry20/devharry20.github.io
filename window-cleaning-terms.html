Window Cleaning Subscription – Terms & Conditions

1. Services Provided

We provide monthly window cleaning services for the property you specify. This covers standard cleaning of exterior and interior windows. Additional work or unusually large properties may incur extra charges, agreed in advance.

2. Payment

  • Payments are collected automatically via Stripe on a monthly basis.
  • Your card will be charged on the same date each month until you cancel.
  • Prices are £25 for smaller properties and £30 for larger properties, unless otherwise agreed.

3. Cancellation

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time by contacting us.
  • Cancellation stops future payments; the current month's payment is non-refundable.

4. Failed Payments

  • If a payment fails, we will attempt to retry.
  • Service may be paused until payment is successfully received.

5. Liability

  • We take care to avoid damage. However, we are not responsible for damage caused by pre-existing conditions, unsafe access, or factors beyond our control (e.g., severe weather).
  • It is your responsibility to ensure the property is safe and accessible for cleaning.

6. Data & Privacy

  • Card details are handled securely by Stripe. We do not store card information.
  • Contact information you provide will only be used to manage your subscription and service.

7. Changes to Terms

We may update these terms occasionally. You will be notified of any changes before they take effect.
