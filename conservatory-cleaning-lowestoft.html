Conservatory Cleaning in Lowestoft

Specialist soft-wash cleaning that protects seals and restores brightness. Standard pressure washing damages conservatories.

The Problem

Conservatory roofs darken with algae, blocking 20-50% of light. Standard pressure washing damages seals, coatings, and mechanisms. Improper cleaning causes expensive leaks and failures.

Why We're Different

We use specialist soft-wash techniques that eliminate algae chemically instead of forcing water through seals. Low-pressure rinsing protects everything while delivering complete cleaning.

What We Protect

  • Glazing seals stay intact and weathertight
  • Protective coatings preserved
  • Opening mechanisms protected
  • Frames and gutters carefully managed

Damage We Prevent

Improper cleaning causes: seal failure (leaks), coating degradation (UV exposure), panel cracks (£500-2,000 to fix), and frame corrosion.

Maintenance Schedule

Heavy shade: annually. Moderate shade: every 18 months. Open sun: every 2-3 years.
